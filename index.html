International conference on "Fostering Digital Transformation for Sustainable Socio-Economic Development"

About Conference

International conference on "Fostering Digital Transformation for Sustainable Socio-Economic Development" is organized by School of Management in Sastra,2022. This conference will be held in hybrid mode allowing registrants the choice to participate in-person or virtually at Sastra University. This creates a forum for researches across the globe ot come together and exchange research ideas. The conference also provides a networking opportunity for researches to collaborate and contribute to the body of knowledge.


The flagship conference will feature a comprehensive high-quality technical program and a vareity of tutorialss and workshops. It will also include an attractive industry program aimed at practitioners, with keynotes and panel discussions from prominent research groups including practicing professionls. One of the key features of the conference will be an innovative exhibits from india including start-ups.

Business Hall, GNV Building, Sastra, Thanjavur, India
